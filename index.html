Telescopic Boom Lift

  • Our fleet includes telescopic boom lifts with a range from 15 to 28 meters, reliable for construction, infrastructure, shipyards, oil & gas, and large industrial sites.
  • Telescopic boom lifts are designed for straight, extended reach at high elevations. They are mainly used where workers need to access hard-to-reach areas over long distances.

Articulated Boom Lift

  • We offer articulated boom lifts with flexible reach from 15 up to 28 meters, ideal for building maintenance, manufacturing plants, utilities, warehousing, and facility management
  • Articulated boom lifts provide flexible access with multiple bending sections, allowing operators to work around obstacles. They are ideal for complex job sites that require precise positioning.
Read More →

Electric Boom Lift

  • Our electric boom lifts, with heights from 13 to 20 meters, are perfect for indoor and environmentally sensitive areas such as airports, malls, hospitals, warehouses, and commercial buildings.
  • These emission-free machines are suited for indoor and environmentally sensitive work areas. They are widely used where low noise and clean operation are essential.
Read More →

Electric & Rough Terrain Scissor Lifts

  • We provide electric scissor lifts for indoor smooth surfaces and rough terrain scissor lifts for outdoor uneven ground, with lifting heights from 8 to 16 meters.
  • Electric scissor lifts are used for indoor tasks on smooth surfaces, offering stable vertical access for maintenance and installation work. Rough terrain scissor lifts are built for outdoor use on uneven ground.
